ACS Courses

  • An Introduction to Scalable Frameworks for Observatory Software Infrastructure
    This course provides a generic introduction to software frameworks and gives the background and the reasons for having one.
    The course itself is not specific to ACS but gives basic concepts and uses ACS as an example.
    It has been given at SPIE - Astronomical Telescopes and Instrumentation in
    Marseille, France, 23-28 June 2008    , in Orlando, Florida, USA, 24-31 May 2006 and in Glasgow, Scotland, UK, 21-25 June 2004 as Short Couse 644.
